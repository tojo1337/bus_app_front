How It Works

Select your desired destination, date, and bus service. Complete the payment process online using your preferred payment method. Once the booking is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email or SMS with your ticket details.

1

Visit Website

Go to the SBSTC official website and select your destination, date of travel, and number of passengers.

2

Select Bus and Seat

Choose from available bus services and select your preferred seat(s).

3

Make Payment

Complete the payment process using your preferred payment method. Once the payment is successful, you will receive a confirmation email or SMS with your e-ticket details.
